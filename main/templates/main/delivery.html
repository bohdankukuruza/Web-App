Delivery Information

Fast, clear, and convenient furniture delivery

At Treasure Chest, we aim to make the final step of your order as smooth as the shopping experience. Learn how delivery works, what to expect after checkout, and how we handle larger furniture items.

Delivery options

How your order reaches you

Small item delivery

Order coordination

Delivery details can be confirmed during order processing so customers understand the next steps clearly.

The goal of our delivery process is simple: customers should always understand what happens after checkout. Treasure Chest presents delivery information in a clear way, helping users feel confident before placing an order.

Important information

Things to keep in mind

Correct delivery details

Please make sure your contact and address information is entered accurately during checkout to avoid unnecessary delivery issues.

Check item dimensions

For larger furniture, it is useful to review product dimensions in advance and ensure the item can fit through entrances and hallways.

Stay reachable

Keeping your phone number up to date helps with order-related communication and delivery coordination.

Inspect your order

Once your items arrive, review them carefully and make sure everything matches the order details.

FAQ

Delivery questions

When will I receive my order?

Delivery timing depends on the item type and order processing stage. Exact details can be confirmed as the order is handled.

Can larger furniture be delivered?

Yes. Larger items are considered separately and prepared with additional attention to handling and transport.

What if my delivery information is incorrect?

Customers should provide accurate details during checkout and update them before order processing whenever necessary.

Can I review my order before confirmation?

Yes. The checkout process allows users to review cart contents and entered information before completing the order.
